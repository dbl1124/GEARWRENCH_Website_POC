# Dark Mode Harmony

GearWrench uses heavy `#000000` pitch black to emphasize luxury and industrial strength.

- **Text on Black**: Never use pure `#FFFFFF` for long body text on a pure black background—it fibrillates and hurts the user's eyes. Use `text-white/90` or `text-gray-300` for readable long-form body copy.
- **Header Text on Black**: Pure white `#FFFFFF` IS acceptable for massive `H1` and `H2` headers to create stark cinematic contrast, but use tracking and uppercase styling aggressively.
- **Borders in Dark Mode**: When stacking black cards on a black background, use extremely subtle borders `border border-white/10` to define physical edges without relying on bright gray colors.
- **Orange on Black**: The GearWrench `#ED8B00` pops beautifully on solid black. Use it for active states, CTA text, and crucial accents, but use it sparingly so it retains its visual power.
